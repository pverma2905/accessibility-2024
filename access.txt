tab:- move forward
shif+tab:- move backward

Turn on NVDA: Ctrl + Alt + N
Turn off NVDA: Insert + Q
Navigate through headings: H (next heading), Shift + H (previous heading)
Navigate through links: K (next link), Shift + K (previous link)
Read the current item: NVDA + Down Arrow
Read the entire page: NVDA + A
Go to the next form field: F (next form element), Shift + F (previous form element)
